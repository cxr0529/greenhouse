History of the greenhouse


Located in the rolling hills of Southwestern Pennsylvania, Mussig's Greenhouse is now a third generation, family owned and operated business. Julian Mussig immigrated to the United States from Germany in hopes of building a better life for himself. After gaining much horticulture experience as an employee in a greenhouse. Julian decided to strike out on his own and established Mussigâ€™s Greenhouse. After successfully operating the business for nearly thirty years, Julian then sold the business to his son, Sonny, in 1965. In 1978, J.E Mussig Greenhouses was incorporated. In 1990, John Mussig, sitting on the Board of Directors for the Pennsylvania Flower Growers Region 4, took over the 31 greenhouse establishment and through hard work and dedication, has continued to see his Grandfather's dream grow. With a fleet of six trucks and a staff of 8 full time employees and up to 18 during peak season, Mussig's Greenhouse Inc. keeps growing to meet the needs of our customers. In the end, a satisfied customer is our best advertisement.
